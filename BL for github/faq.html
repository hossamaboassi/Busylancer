BusyLancer is a platform that connects businesses needing on-site temporary staff with verified local freelancers (BusyLancers) for short-term gigs, events, and tasks in the real world.

Unlike traditional platforms focused on online work, BusyLancer specializes in **on-site, real-world jobs**. We also prioritize speed, local matching, and offer unique features like verified profiles and quick payments for gigs ranging from a few hours to several days.

BusyLancer is for two main groups: **Businesses** (event organizers, retailers, marketing agencies, etc.) who need temporary staff for on-site tasks, and **BusyLancers** (freelancers, students, part-timers, job seekers) looking for flexible, paid, real-world work.

Currently, BusyLancer is focused on the Saudi Arabian market, with a pilot program launching in Riyadh. We plan to expand to other cities and regions in the MENA area in the future.

For BusyLancers, joining and finding gigs is free. For businesses, we operate on a flexible pricing model depending on the type and duration of the gig. Transparent fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any booking.

Sign up on our platform, complete your profile, and pass our verification process. Once approved, you can start browsing and applying for gigs in your area!

We focus on on-site temporary gigs, including event staff, promoters, retail assistants, photographers, videographers, ushers, and more. Jobs are typically short-term, from a few hours to several days.

Payments are processed securely through the BusyLancer platform. Once a gig is completed and approved by the business, funds are typically released to your linked bank account or digital wallet within 24-48 hours, depending on your bank's processing times.

After registering your business profile, simply click "Post a Job" on your dashboard. Follow the steps to describe your gig, set requirements, and specify the location and payment. Our platform will then match you with suitable BusyLancers.

BusyLancer is designed for speed. Our smart matching system can connect you with available BusyLancers in minutes, often allowing you to confirm staff for your gig in under an hour for urgent needs. Of course, longer lead times are always recommended for complex roles.

BusyLancer supports major credit/debit cards and local payment methods (such as Mada, Apple Pay, STC Pay, etc. in Saudi Arabia). All transactions are securely handled through our platform to protect both businesses and BusyLancers.

Our platform has clear policies for cancellations and disputes, designed to be fair to both parties. In case of issues, our support team is available to mediate and resolve problems efficiently. Please refer to our detailed Terms of Service for specific guidelines.

We implement a multi-layered verification process for all BusyLancers, including ID checks and background screenings where applicable. Our rating and review system ensures transparency, allowing both businesses and BusyLancers to build a reputation based on performance and reliability.

Our platform includes mechanisms to track gig attendance and ensure timely payments. In such rare cases, our support team investigates thoroughly and takes appropriate action, including payment protection for BusyLancers and penalties for non-compliant users as per our terms.

Our Terms of Service outline the rules and guidelines for using the BusyLancer platform, including user responsibilities, payment terms, dispute resolution, and intellectual property. We encourage all users to read them carefully before using our services.

Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and transparency in our data practices. It details your rights regarding your information on our platform.
